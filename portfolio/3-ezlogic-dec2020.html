EZLOGIC is a tutorial website that covers the fundamental discrete mathematics topic such as logic and boolean algebra. It was made to organize all the notes taken from the different classes for easier and more interactive studying as the site featured examples within subtopics as well as short quizzes at the end of the topic to assess one's learning.
