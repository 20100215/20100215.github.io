GROCELIST - Online Grocery Shopping App (Prototype)

Project information

  • Category: Mobile Application Prototype
  • Project Date: December 2020
  • Project Link: [Click here]

GROCELIST is a mobile application that allows its users to search around and purchase their groceries online, saving them effort and time used to shop physically in grocery stores and avoid possible health dangers amidst the Covid-19 pandemic. Its main features include item browsing by category, viewing of product info, scanning of items, creation and editing of shopping lists, and checkout and delivery.
